Class Groups

Kids

Our school is a solution for families who would like to expose their children to the world of Chess.Join us as we embark on a chess adventure filled with colorful pieces, engaging puzzles, and the joy of learning. Checkmate boredom, and let the chess fun begin!

Teens

Our programs blend learning and excitement, offering challenging strategies and tactics to keep you engaged. Whether you're a casual player or aspiring to compete at a higher level, our dynamic approach ensures chess remains both thrilling and intellectually stimulating.

Adults

Enjoy a relaxed and supportive environment as you hone your skills, meet fellow enthusiasts, and discover the timeless beauty of the game. Unleash your inner grandmaster – it's your move!

Private

For those seeking a personalized chess experience, our private coaching at win-warriors Chess Academy is tailored just for you. Benefit from one-on-one sessions with expert coaches who will analyze your gameplay, identify strengths and weaknesses, and craft a customized training plan.

We Also Offer

Beginner

Our beginner's program focuses on introducing you to the fundamentals of chess, from understanding the chessboard and pieces to basic opening strategies. We'll guide you through the rules, tactics, and help you build a strong foundation for your chess skills.

Intermediate

Ready to take your chess game to the next level? Our intermediate program is designed for players who have a grasp of the basics and are looking to deepen their understanding of strategy and tactics.

Experienced

Dive into complex endgames, master advanced opening variations, and develop a deeper understanding of strategic concepts. Our expert coaches will provide personalized guidance to enhance your game, analyze your strengths and weaknesses, and prepare you for challenging opponents.

Professionals

Refine your opening repertoire, work on nuanced positional play, and engage in high-level strategic discussions. Our team of seasoned professionals is dedicated to helping you reach your full potential, providing insights, and supporting your journey to becoming a top-tier chess player.
